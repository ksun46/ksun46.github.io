About Me

Short Bio

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Operations Research at Georgia Tech, working with Dr. Andy Sun. I am interested in the theory and application of optimization in a broad sense. I am currently working on distributed and decomposition algorithms for nonconvex optimization, which find their applications in large-scale continuous and discrete problems arising from engineering, especially power systems, and data science.

Before joining the Ph.D. program, I received my B.S. degrees in Industrial Engineering and Mathematics from the same institute.
