About Me

Short Bio

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Operations Research at Georgia Tech, working with Prof. X. Andy Sun. Before joining the Ph.D. program, I received my B.S. degrees in Industrial Engineering and Mathematics from the same institute.

Broadly speaking, my research focuses on the theory and application of optimization. I am currently working on distributed and decomposition algorithms for large-scale continuous and discrete optimization problems with convergence guarantees. I am also interested in efficient and practical solution methods for nonconvex nonsmooth problems arising from data science and engineering fields.
